"How to Make a Peanut Butter and Banana Sandwich!"

Necessities:
  • 2 slices of bread
  • peanut butter
  • 1 banana
  • a knife
  • OPTIONAL: a toaster, toaster oven, or grill
Instructions:
  1. Spread peanut butter across the two slices of bread.
  2. Chop the banana into how ever many slices you would like.
  3. Lay the banana slices across one or both of the slices of bread.
  4. Put the two slices of bread "peanut butter side in" together.
  5. OPTIONAL: Toast or grill the sandwich to make it melty and delicious!
